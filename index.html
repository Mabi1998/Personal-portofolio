About Me

My name is Mabintou Ouattara


I am a recent graduate of a Bachelor of Science in Petroleum Engineering & a Mathematics minor. I possess the Engineer In Training (E.I.T) certification from the Association of Professional Engineers and Geoscientists of Alberta (APEGA), and I am eligible to work for any employers in Canada without sponsorship.


I am passionate about volunteering, STEM outreach, mentorship and education, and I frequently participate in multiple speaking engagements to inspire youths about STEM. While attending school, I have worked and volunteered with multiple organizations that promote STEM to the younger generations, especially young girls. I am a strong advocate for females in STEM, and I take actions to boost their contribution to the Science and Technology fields.


While working on expanding my skills, I developped a passion for programming. I am an aspiring web developper, and I possess intermediate skills in designing a responsive website using the HyperText Markup Language (HTML) and Cascading Styling Sheet (CSS) languages.


I am originally from the Ivory Coast, West Africa, and I speak 3 languages fluently. My main hobbies are volunteering, and exploring new horizons. I love travelling around the world, learning new cultures and languages.
